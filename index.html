two lines about dosa Dosa is a savory, crispy pancake originating from South India, made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It's often served with a variety of chutneys and sambar, making it a popular breakfast item and a delightful snack. 🌟

two lines about pani puri Pani Puri is a beloved Indian street food, featuring hollow, crispy puris filled with tangy tamarind water, spicy mashed potatoes, and chickpeas. This delightful snack offers a burst of flavors and textures with every bite, making it a crowd favorite. 🤤

two lines about fish fry and white rice Fish fry and white rice make a classic, comforting meal in many coastal regions. The crispy, seasoned fish pairs perfectly with the simplicity of steamed rice, creating a balanced and flavorful dish. 🍚🐟

two lines about poori Poori is a deep-fried Indian bread, made from whole wheat flour, that puffs up into golden, crispy rounds. It's typically enjoyed with savory curries, adding a delightful crunch to any meal. 🥘🍞
